Welcome to PoeTea ✨

PoeTea (pronounced [po-eh-tea]) is an interactive recommendation system for tea and poetry!

You'll tell us:

(1) What caffeine content you'd like your tea to have

(2) Up to three flavor words that describe your ideal tea taste

(3) A word or phrase you'd like to see in a poem

And our system will provide you with:

(1) New tea(s) to try!

(2) A fun poem
